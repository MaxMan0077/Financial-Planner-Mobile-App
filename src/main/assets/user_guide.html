User Guide

Getting Started

This application is made to allow you to take control of your personal and household expenses and work out ways to budget effectively

To start you will want to head to the registration page to set up an account (note passwords must be more than 8 characters and contain both letters and numbers)

Homepage

Once logged in you will be taken to the apps homepage. This page will show you a pie chart showing all your expenses for the current month as well as showing your ratio of income to expenditure at a bar at the bottom of the screen.

You can use the navbar to navigate across the apps 4 main pages: Income, Expenses, Savings Goals and the homepage

Income

Pressing the income icon on the navbar will show you this screen which will allows you to view your current income sources. You can swipe left on each income source to delete it.

You can also add an income source by pressing the plus button. This page will allow you to enter the name of an income source, set its amount and set weather it is a monthly income or a one off payment (Note: you receive a notification at the start of each month letting you know that all non monthly income sources will be removed from your income sources list)

Expenses

Pressing the expenses icon on the navbar will show you this screen which will allow you to view all your current monthly expenses and their respective amounts. You can swipe left on each expense to delete it

You can also add an expense by pressing the plus button. This page will allow you to enter the name of the expense, set its amount and set weather it is a monthly expense or a one off payment (Note: you receive a notification at the start of each month letting you know that all non monthly expenses will be removed from your expenses list)

Savings Goals

Pressing the savings goals icon on the navbar will take you to this screen where you can list your current savings goals. You can swipe left on each savings goal to delete it.

By tapping on a savings goal you can see the details about it such as the cost of the item, when you are aiming to purchase the item and how much you are needing to save per month (Note: The savings per month for all savings goal will be added to your list of expenses)

By pressing the plus button you can add a savings goal where you are taken to this page where you are asked to enter the name of the item, how much it costs and select the date of when you wish to purchase the item.
